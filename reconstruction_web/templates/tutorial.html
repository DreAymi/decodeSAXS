Tutorial

1.You need to upload your SAXS data, it must be ascii text file (DAT*), or GNOM (.out) file.

  If you upload (.out) file, you don't need to give estimated maximum radius(Å).

  If you upload (DAT*) file, you can give an estimated maximum radius(Å). If not, our project will fit one.

2.After you submit your job, you will get a Job ID, you must remember this Job ID. you can use this Job ID to check (or download) your result on check page.

3.You can check your job status (running or finished) in history page based on your jobname.

4.You also can write your email address, this is used to send results after the reconstruction is finished,

  but sometimes it doesn't work. So make sure you remember your job ID after your submission.

5.Any questions or suggestions you can contact with us through email: hehao1777@163.com.
